A Few Words About Me

I am a Senior Marketing student at the University of Northern Colordo, pursuing a minor in Digital Marketing. I aspire to have a career in graphic design as well as marketing. My goal is to find a career that makes me happy but also pays the bills. With this career, I'm going to help my family and live the life my parents and I have aspired for me.

Career Interests

I want to find a job that relates to my interests but also still challenges me eductionally and logically. Having a job that uses graphic design and web design would allow me to use my knowledge to better another company. I would also like my career to teach me, expanding my education and knowledge of how this business works in the real world.

Personal Interests

Aside from school and a career, I want to be comfortable, I want to be able to do spur of the moment things without worring about money. I want to have date nights, family dinners, have beach days, etc. I want work and life to be separate, but I want to happy doing both.
